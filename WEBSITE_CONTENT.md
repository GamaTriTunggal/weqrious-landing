## 2. Problem Statement

**Heading:** Counterfeiting Is a Revenue Killer. Passive Labels Won't Save You.

**Body:**
Global counterfeiting costs businesses over $500 billion a year in lost revenue, brand damage, and consumer safety risks. Traditional hologram stickers and static QR codes offer no real-time intelligence — they can't tell you when a fake surfaces, where it was scanned, or who scanned it.

Worse, every product scan is a missed opportunity. Millions of consumers interact with your packaging daily, yet most brands capture zero data from those moments.

WEQRIOUS changes both. We turn every scan into a verification event and every verification into an engagement opportunity — giving you protection and insight in a single platform.

## 3. How It Works

**Heading:** Three Steps. Full Protection.

**Step 1 — Generate & Print**
Create unique QR codes for every product unit or batch. Configure validation pages, warranty forms, and campaign experiences — all from one dashboard. Order professional printing or integrate with your existing production line.

**Step 2 — Consumers Scan to Verify**
Customers scan the QR code with any smartphone camera. Instantly, they see whether the product is authentic — complete with your branding, product details, and certification badges. No app download required.

**Step 3 — Engage, Protect, Analyze**
Each scan feeds real-time data into your dashboard: location heatmaps, counterfeit alerts, warranty registrations, and campaign participation. Suspicious patterns are flagged automatically so you can act fast.

## 8. FAQ

**Heading:** Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How quickly can we deploy WEQRIOUS for our products?**
A: Most brands go live within a week. Sign up, create your products, generate QR codes, and print. No hardware installation or app development required — consumers scan with any smartphone camera.

**Q: Is the platform customizable to match our brand identity?**
A: Fully. Every consumer-facing page — validation, warranty, campaign — is white-labeled with your logo, colors, and imagery. You can create multiple templates and schedule seasonal themes.

**Q: How does counterfeit detection work without specialized hardware?**
A: WEQRIOUS uses intelligent software-based detection. Each QR code is unique and tracked. The system flags anomalies like excessive scans, impossible-travel patterns (same code scanned in two distant cities within minutes), and products appearing outside their authorized distribution zones.

**Q: Is our data secure?**
A: Security is built into every layer. We use JWT-based authentication with HttpOnly cookies, tenant-isolated data architecture, full audit logging, and infrastructure hardened following industry best practices. Your data is never shared between tenants.
