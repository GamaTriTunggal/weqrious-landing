Counterfeiting Is a Revenue Killer. Passive Labels Won't Save You.

Global counterfeiting costs businesses over $500 billion a year in lost revenue, brand damage, and consumer safety risks. Traditional hologram stickers and static QR codes offer no real-time intelligence — they can't tell you when a fake surfaces, where it was scanned, or who scanned it.

Worse, every product scan is a missed opportunity. Millions of consumers interact with your packaging daily, yet most brands capture zero data from those moments.

WEQRIOUS changes both. We turn every scan into a verification event and every verification into an engagement opportunity — giving you protection and insight in a single platform.

Three Steps. Full Protection.

1

Generate & Print

Create unique QR codes for every product unit or batch. Configure validation pages, warranty forms, and campaign experiences — all from one dashboard. Order professional printing or integrate with your existing production line.

2

Consumers Scan to Verify

Customers scan the QR code with any smartphone camera. Instantly, they see whether the product is authentic — complete with your branding, product details, and certification badges. No app download required.

3

Engage, Protect, Analyze

Each scan feeds real-time data into your dashboard: location heatmaps, counterfeit alerts, warranty registrations, and campaign participation. Suspicious patterns are flagged automatically so you can act fast.
